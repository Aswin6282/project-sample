Perfume is a mixture of fragrant essential oils, aroma compounds, and solvents used to give a pleasant scent to the body or living spaces. The word comes from the Latin per fumum, meaning "through smoke." Fragrances are categorized by their concentration of perfume oil, which determines their strength and longevity.
